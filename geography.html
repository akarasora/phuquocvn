Geography

Phu Quoc is located quietly away from southwest Vietnam and is part of Kien Giang province. On this beautiful island, visitors get to observe the majestic giant mountains together with the green forests. Even though the island is surrounded by sea, the water level around the island is quite shallow, less than 10 meters deep, so visitors will not be worried about the depth of the sea. While the climate is divided into dry and rainy seasons, it does not affect much of the local life because the temperature is quite warm. The beach stretches from north to south, and at the southern end lies a vibrant village where tourists are going to get an opportunity to discover during their visit. At the same time, this tropical island has a lot of stunning waterfalls and beautiful scenery, where you can observe the unique creatures and go hiking.
