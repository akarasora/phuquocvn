People

Hospitable, chastity, and amiable are words that are used to describe the people in Phu Quoc. Fishermen and other residents sometimes serve as guides for tourists. Normally, Vietnamese on the mainland do not speak English frequently; however, this is not the case in Phu Quoc. Since tourists are everywhere, most locals do know how to communicate in English. For example, people can find a fisherman or a farmer having conversations with foreigners. Despite the fact that many individuals from other provinces come to work and live on Phu Quoc Island, the locals here are extremely grateful for the contribution of the Chinese-Vietnamese community. They have helped by developing several wonderful and traditional foods for Phu Quoc services.
