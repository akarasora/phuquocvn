Economy

An ideal location for investors. Aside from forests, other resources such as land, sea, and freshwater offer great potential for economic development. Phu Quoc is well-known for its green peppers and fish sauce; these two businesses have always been highly successful. Furthermore, tourism activities have grown significantly, allowing residents to deliver a wide range of services. With over hundreds of lodgings and hotels, as well as having such wonder activities, Phu Quoc has welcomed millions of tourists throughout the year. This destination is always the best idea for a vacation. During COVID-19, Phu Quoc struggled because visitor revenue had dropped. On the other hand, real estate investors performed better as it continued to rise during the pandemic.
